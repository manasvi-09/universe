About the Universe

The Universe is a vast, mysterious expanse of space, time, matter, and energy. It includes everything that exists—planets, stars, galaxies, black holes, and even the invisible dark matter and dark energy. Born from the Big Bang roughly 13.8 billion years ago, the Universe continues to expand and evolve. Scientists use telescopes and satellites to explore its wonders and understand its origins. With each discovery, we unlock more about the nature of existence and our place within this cosmic masterpiece.
